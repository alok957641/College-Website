Degree Courses

BN College Bhagalpur offers 4-year degree programs in Arts, Science, and Commerce for students after 12th.
These courses build academic depth and career readiness.

B.A. (Bachelor of Arts)

Ideal for students interested in humanities and social sciences. Subjects include History, Political Science, Hindi, English, Philosophy, and more.

B.Sc. (Bachelor of Science)

Designed for science enthusiasts aiming for careers in research, medicine, or technology. Includes Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Botany, and Zoology.

B.Com (Bachelor of Commerce)

Focused on business, finance, and accounting. Perfect for students planning careers in commerce, banking, or entrepreneurship.

Vocational Courses

BN College Bhagalpur offers skill-based vocational programs designed for job-readiness and industry relevance.
These 3-year courses are ideal for students seeking practical careers after 12th.

BCA
(Bachelor of Computer Applications)

Focused on programming, web development, and IT skills. Perfect for students aiming for careers in software and tech industries.

BBA
(Bachelor of Business Administration)

Focused on business management, marketing, and entrepreneurship. Great for students aiming for corporate roles or MBA studies.

OMSP
(Office Management & Secretarial Practices)

Designed for careers in office administration, documentation, and executive support. Learn typing, shorthand, and business communication.
